# Welcome to ProjetosVoluntariosTech

ProjetosVoluntariosTech is a volunteer-driven initiative that uses technology, knowledge, and personal resources to create value in local communities. We empower microentrepreneurs, educational institutions, and community organizations by building practical digital solutions.

## Our Mission

Our goal is to make technology accessible to everyone, regardless of technical background. We believe that digital transformation should reach every corner of the community, from small businesses to educational institutions.

## Key Features

- **Beginner-Friendly**: Perfect for first-time contributors and those learning web development
- **Real Projects**: Work on actual solutions used by real organizations
- **Educational Focus**: Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern web development
- **Community Impact**: See your work make a difference in local communities
- **Volunteer-Driven**: Built by volunteers, for the community

## Current Projects

We've successfully delivered projects including:

- **Escola Bíblica Portal**: An interactive Bible school platform with calendar, quizzes, and rankings
- **Solução Refrigeração**: A professional business website for a local refrigeration service
