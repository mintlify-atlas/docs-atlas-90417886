## Welcome to ProjetosVoluntariosTech

We're excited that you're interested in joining our community! ProjetosVoluntariosTech is a volunteer-driven initiative that uses technology, knowledge, and resources to create value in local communities. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your tech journey, there's a place for you here.

<Note>
Our mission is to make technology accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill level. We believe that everyone can contribute and learn together.
</Note>

## Why Join Us?

By joining ProjetosVoluntariosTech, you'll:

- **Make Real Impact**: Contribute to projects that directly benefit local communities
- **Learn and Grow**: Access educational resources and learn from experienced community members
- **Build Your Portfolio**: Work on real-world projects that showcase your skills
- **Connect with Others**: Meet like-minded volunteers passionate about tech for good
- **Give Back**: Use your skills to help educational and community initiatives

## Getting Started

### 1. Explore Our Projects

Visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Yudikom/ProjetosVoluntariosTech) to see what we're working on. Browse through:

- **Open Issues**: Find tasks that match your skills and interests
- **Project Documentation**: Learn about our current initiatives
- **Contribution Guidelines**: Understand how to contribute effectively

### 2. Connect with the Community

<Info>
We welcome community members from all backgrounds and locations. Communication primarily happens through GitHub, but we're building more channels to connect.
</Info>

Join our communication channels:

- **GitHub Discussions**: Ask questions, share ideas, and connect with other contributors
- **GitHub Issues**: Report bugs, suggest features, or pick up tasks
- **Social Media**: Follow us for updates and announcements

### 3. Make Your First Contribution

Ready to dive in? Here's how:

1. **Fork the repository** on GitHub
2. **Choose an issue** labeled `good-first-issue` or `beginner`
3. **Make your changes** and test them thoroughly
4. **Submit a pull request** with a clear description
5. **Engage with feedback** from maintainers and community members

<Tip>
Don't be afraid to ask questions! Everyone was a beginner once, and our community is here to support you.
</Tip>

## Community Values

Our community is built on these core values:

### Inclusivity
We welcome people of all backgrounds, skill levels, and experiences. Everyone deserves to learn and contribute.

### Collaboration
We work together, share knowledge freely, and support each other's growth.

### Impact
We focus on creating real value for communities through technology.

### Learning
We embrace mistakes as learning opportunities and encourage continuous improvement.

### Respect
We treat everyone with kindness and respect, fostering a safe and welcoming environment.

## Who Can Join?

**Everyone!** Whether you're:

- A developer looking to use your skills for good
- A designer wanting to create impactful user experiences
- A student eager to learn and build your portfolio
- An educator interested in educational technology
- A community organizer wanting to leverage technology
- Someone curious about tech with no experience yet

You're welcome here!

## What to Expect

As a community member, you can expect:

- **Support and Mentorship**: More experienced members helping newcomers
- **Regular Updates**: Information about new projects and opportunities
- **Recognition**: Credit for your contributions and achievements
- **Growth Opportunities**: Chances to take on new challenges and responsibilities
- **Friendly Atmosphere**: A welcoming, judgment-free space to learn and create
